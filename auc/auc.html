Afri Union Coin

  • AUC coin has the value of the currency used in the public market of Africa and will be the
  • standard for exchanging various types of currencies and services.
  • AUC coin is issued based on ERC20 to be listed on AUC’s exchange and other contracted exchanges.
  • It was designed with the purpose of middle transactions in international border transactions between African countries.
